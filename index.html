Visit Edinburgh

Edinburgh, the capital of Scotland, and one of its 32 council areas, is renowned for its rich history, cultural festivals, and architectural splendor.

Places To Visit

Edinburgh Castle

One of the oldest fortified places in Europe, with a long rich history as a royal residence, military garrison, prison and fortress.

Scott Monument

Victorian Gothic monument to Scottish author Sir Walter Scott.

St. Anthony's Chapel Ruins

The only structure in the central area of Holyrood Park, on the north side of Arthur's Seat.

National Museum of Scotland

Home of collections relating to Scottish antiquities, culture and history.
